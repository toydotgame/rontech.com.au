Welcome to Rontech Security Industries Pty Ltd
Rontech Security is a 100% Australian owned and operated company established in 1986.

The world we are living in today, Intruder Alarm systems, CCTV Surveillance systems and Access Control Systems are becoming more and more part of our everyday life.  Rontech Security has designed, installed, serviced and maintained numerous Intruder Alarm systems, CCTV Surveillance systems and Access Control systems for major corporations, governments, major projects, commercial, industrial and residential customers throughout Australian over the past twenty years and we continue to do so.

Rontech Security offers quality retail security solutions by providing professional loss prevention devices and systems to protect your assets and providing a duty of care to your staff and personnel.  Rontech is an Australian leader in electronic retail security solutions.

If you are looking for home security solutions, we can offer unbiased professional advice on the leading home security products.  Rontech delivers quality home security systems to suit your individual requirements and budget.

Together with our technology partners, Rontech can provide “State of the Art” Intruder Detection Alarm systems, Home Security, Retail Security, CCTV Surveillance and Access Control security solutions tailored to meet your individual application.

Rontech Security provides a nationwide premium service to individuals, businesses, companies and governments, who require professional high performance security solutions.

We achieve this by offering our customers
  • ASIAL Membership.
  • ASIAL approved Grade A1 Monitoring Station (owned and operated).
  • Australian Standards Compliance.
  • Utilising "state of the art" technology.
  • Providing experienced and qualified personnel.
  • Having extensive expertise in providing security design and solutions to the commercial, government, industrial, retail and residential sectors.
  • Provide a confidential and personal customer service.
  • We value the human factor. No automated telecommunications prompting systems at Rontech Security.

ASIAL

In promoting the ongoing professionalism demanded within the Security Industry, we are pleased to advise that Rontech Security is an active and current member of the Australian Security Industry Association Limited.

ASIAL is the spokesperson and self-regulatory body of the Security Industry and its membership requirements inherently involve a high level of business ethics, standards and industrial award compliance.
